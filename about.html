About Pixel Planets

Pixel Planets is a web-based interactive experience that allows users to create and customize their own pixel art planets. Users can choose from a variety of biomes, atmospheres, and features to design unique worlds. The planets are procedurally generated, meaning each planet is one-of-a-kind. Users can save and share their creations with others, as well as explore planets created by the community.
